About us
GT Movie Store is a website dedicated to movies. Users of the site can search through our database to find their favorite movies. Once they have found a movie, they can learn about it through the posted description and post their own reviews. These reviews can be viewed by all users of the site, but keep it civil! Admins of the site can edit and delete users' reviews. Users can also buy movies. While viewing a movie, if a user decides they would like to purchase it they can add it to their cart. At any point users can view their current cart and once they are satisfied with the movies in their cart they can check out.
